Good to know

FAQ

What is the dress code?+

We invite you to wear summer formal attire, elevated, celebratory, and ready for a night of dancing. The ceremony is in a church, and the reception will be by the water, so we recommend layers and comfortable shoes.

Should I bring a car?+

Yes, we recommend bringing a car. Drummond Island is best explored on your own schedule, and you’ll need to take the ferry from DeTour Village to reach the island. There are no Ubers on the island!

What do I need to know about the ferry?+

The Drummond Island Ferry runs year round. Please check the schedule before traveling and allow extra time during busy summer periods or changing weather. The Drummond Island Ferry is a car ferry, town is about 10 miles from the dock, please plan accordingly!

Where should I stay?+

Visit our Places To Stay section for cabin rentals, hotel options, and vacation-rental links. We recommend booking early for a July weekend.

Are children invited?+

We love your little ones; however, our celebration will be adults only unless a family is specifically named on your invitation.

What happens if it rains?+

We are celebrating rain or shine! Please check the forecast before you travel and bring a layer for an island evening. The ceremony will be indoors, and the reception venue is indoor/outdoor if necessary we will move cocktail hour inside.

When should I RSVP?+

Please respond by the date printed on your invitation so we can finalize our celebration. Invitations will be sent out this spring.
